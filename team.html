Residents, Fellows & Postdocs

Harman Brah, M.D.

He graduated Phi Beta Kappa from Loyola Marymount University and earned his medical degree from the University of California, San Diego School of Medicine. He has research experience in pharmaceutical chemistry, structural biophysics, and pharmacodynamics. His current interests include the metabolic, genetic, and immunologic basis of psychosis, as well as the use of large language models (LLMs) in psychiatry. He is currently a research track resident at the UCLA/VA Psychiatry Residency Program.
