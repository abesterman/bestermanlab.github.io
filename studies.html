Our Studies

Mental Health Crises in Youth with Intellectual and Developmental Disabilities

Studying clinical, psychosocial, and genetic factors associated with mental health crises in youth with IDDs. Clinical Trial

Multi-omics of Early-Onset Psychosis

Using whole genome sequencing in collaboration with RCIGM and autoantibody profiling in collaboration with Dr. Christopher Bartley at Translational Immunopsychiatry Unit (TIU) @ NIMH to study immunogenomic origions of psychosis in youth. Clinical Trial

Immunogenomics of Pediatric Catatonia

Using whole genome sequencing in collaboration with RCIGM and autoantibody profiling in collaboration with Dr. Christopher Bartley at Translational Immunopsychiatry Unit (TIU) @ NIMH to study immunogenomic origions of catatonia in youth.

Feasibility of Wearable Devices in Autism Spectrum Disorders

With Dr. Benjamin Smarr @ UCSD Bioengineering, evaluating the feasability of various wearable devices for youth with ASD

The Microbiome in Adolescent Antidepressant Treatment Response

With Dr. Rob Knight, researching the role of the gut microbiome in treament response to Prozac for youth with Depression

Immunophenotyping of Down Syndrome Regression Disorder

Collaborating on a study led by Dr. John Santoro @ CHLA, Dr. Gena Mooneyham and Dr. Christopher Bartley @NIH examining the role of neuroinflammation in Down Syndreome Regression Disorder

Clinical Legal and Ethical Considerations in Pediatric Threat Assessment

In collaboration with Dr. Bonnie Kaiser @ UCSD Anthropology and Dr. Dov Fox and USD School of Law, we are using mixed-method surveying to assess how psychiatrists make clinical decisions about threat assessment in youth
